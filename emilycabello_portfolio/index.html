Emily Cabello

Hi, my name is Emily Cabello. I'm a senior visual communication design major interested in working in brand identity creation and marketing. I'd love to one day be a successful freelance designer, although I want to first gain a better understanding of the industry by first working at different companies or design studios. I'm from Santa Ana, California, and have been living in San Francisco for two years. My time outside of school and work is spent with my cat, crocheting, and exploring other art mediums; currently I'm into fiber arts and trying to do my own nails.

My interest in graphic design began when I signed up for a yearbook production class in middle school! I really enjoyed exploring visual storytelling, and after switching between majors for my first few years at State, I decided I wanted to pursue it as a career. Something I've been working on improving upon lately is my typographic design skills.
