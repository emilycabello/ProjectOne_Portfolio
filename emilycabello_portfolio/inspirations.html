Inspirations

  • Susan Kare


    • Susan Kare's work designing the apple icon suite inspires me because it is a perfect example of efficient visual communication. She was able to simplify everyday items and assign meaning to them in a timeless way.


  • Julia Yus


    • Julia Yus is a paper artist and engineer. Her work inspires me as she is pushing boundaries for hand crafts to be viewed as art. Seeing someone who mostly works with a niche art style succeed with both commercial and personal projects is inspiring as someone who wants to incorportate her own hand craft (crochet) into design.


  • Jonna Mayer


    • Jonna Mayer's bold typogrpahic range inspires me to think outside the box for my designs. She's also collaborated with some musical artists I love like Lorde, Shygirl, and Pinkpanthress. Her creative direction on these projects perfectly captures and enhances the artists' style.
